# PlanKept Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-04-17  
Version: 1.0.0

This Privacy Policy explains how PlanKept processes personal information when
you use the PlanKept pages on this site, including the public app page under
`/apps/PlanKept/`, support channels, wish-list flow, and any future app
experience that links to this policy.

PlanKept is currently in an early public stage. Its page on this personal site
is live now as the public support, legal, and interest-signup surface.

Contact:

- Email: `alekgameshelp2@gmail.com`
- Support page: `/support/`

## Summary

- The current website is intentionally lightweight.
- We may receive the information you choose to send through support email or
  wish-list email.
- If you click through to an external placeholder form or external email
  service, that third party may also process your information under its own
  terms and privacy policy.
- We do not sell your personal data.
- If the future app introduces additional data processing, this policy should be
  updated before or with that change.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect or receive:

- Your email address if you contact support or join the wish list by email
- Your name or nickname if you choose to include it
- The message or notes you send us
- Basic technical request information normally involved in website delivery,
  such as IP address, browser type, and request timing

If a future PlanKept app links to this policy, additional categories may be
added here before those features are launched or materially changed.

## 2. How We Use Information

We may use information to:

- respond to support requests
- keep track of early interest in the product
- improve the website and launch flow
- protect the service and investigate misuse
- comply with legal obligations

## 3. Legal Bases

Where applicable, we rely on one or more of these grounds:

- your consent, where you choose to contact us or submit information
- performance of a requested service, such as responding to support
- legitimate interests, such as maintaining and improving the website
- compliance with legal obligations

## 4. Sharing

We may share information with service providers that help operate the site or
communications flow, such as:

- website hosting providers
- email providers
- any external placeholder form or list service you intentionally use

We may also disclose information if required by law or if reasonably necessary
to protect rights, safety, or the service.

## 5. Retention

We keep personal information only as long as reasonably necessary for support,
wish-list management, legal compliance, or legitimate business operations.

Because PlanKept is still early, retention practices may be simplified for now.
If retention becomes more complex later, this policy should be updated.

## 6. Data Security

We use reasonable measures intended to protect information, but no method of
storage or transmission is perfectly secure.

## 7. International Transfers

If our service providers process information in other countries, your
information may be transferred internationally and handled according to the
provider's safeguards and legal requirements.

## 8. Children's Privacy

PlanKept is not intended for children under 16, and we do not knowingly seek to
collect personal information from children under 16.
